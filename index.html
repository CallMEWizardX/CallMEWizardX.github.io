About NGO Sahyog

Sahyog is a non-profit and non-governmental organization committed to enhancing the living standards of the underprivileged section of society through our awareness programs and events. Our NGO is working in the field of education, health care, and livelihood.

The ultimate aim of Sahyog is to support the underprivileged section of society by improving their lives. We work at the forefront to reshape the lives of millions.

Education

Education is the most powerful weapon for fighting poverty. Our organization is working towards empowering of underprivileged children and youth by unleashing the power of knowledge.

Healthcare

We have been working towards making quality health care services assessable to the poor and marginalized communities.

Livelihood

Our NGO is working closely with both the private and public sectors for creating an inclusive and at the same time sustainable livelihood for communities across India.

Our Work

The Sahyog focuses towards the development of the potential of marginalized children and women. We have been driving equitable transformation through quality healthcare, all-inclusive education, gender-equitable & sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Tree Plantation

We passionately promote tree plantation to restore ecosystems, combat climate change, and enhance biodiversity. Join us in preserving nature's gifts for a sustainable future filled with clean air, thriving wildlife, and a healthier planet.

Blood Donation Camp

We organize blood donation camps, saving lives one donation at a time. Join us in this noble cause, giving hope and support to those in need, and making a lasting impact on our community's health.

Social Awareness

Through various initiatives, we tirelessly spread awareness about critical issues like women empowerment, mental health, and environmental conservation. Together, we can ignite change, empower others, and create a more compassionate and sustainable world.
